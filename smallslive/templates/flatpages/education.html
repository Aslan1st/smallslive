Education

Education is a key component to the mission of the SmallsLIVE Foundation. Our audio/video archive is a unique resource for jazz music, documenting the work of nearly 4000 musicians over twelve consecutive years to date. With currently more than 15,000 shows from both Smalls and Mezzrow, the archive is growing at a rate of 35 new shows added weekly.

“The entire New School community is thrilled to be working with the SmallsLIVE Foundation on this project. The SmallsLIVE class is an important part of the rethinking of musicology at the School of Jazz & Contemporary Music, where students develop their skills in critical thinking, research, and placing art in an historical and social context through the lens of living musical cultures.”

Keller Colker, Dean of the Jazz & Contemporary Music Dept. at The New School.

School

The School of Jazz & Contemporary Music at The New School in New York City, under the direction of Keller Coker, has given SmallsLIVE the unique opportunity to create and teach a course with the SmallsLIVE archive as the centerpiece. Our goal is to familiarize students with current artists and their relation to Smalls Jazz Club and the current scene.

Class

This class, titled “SmallsLIVE: Profiles From The Contemporary Jazz Scene”, is already in its second year.  Students use the SmallsLIVE archive to research specific, currently performing; artists associated with Smalls, and get an insight into their musical thought process.  The students work in groups and then present their work to the class through transcriptions, interviews and concert reviews. In many cases, the artists themselves are brought to the class to speak directly with the students and to engage in dialogue. The SmallsLIVE Foundation subsidizes the expense of creating and operating this class.

The SmallsLIVE Foundation is available to any school music program that is interested in our archive or educational initiative. We are available for school tours and private presentations as well as workshops and lessons. Please email us at education@smallslive.com
