Become a member of SmallsLIVE and access our live streamed shows nightly!

Become a subscribing member and gain unlimited access to our extensive audio/video archive of past shows. By becoming a subscribing member you are directly supporting the musicians and the SmallsLIVE Revenue Share Project.

The SmallsLIVE Archive is a library of every show (audio and video) performed at Smalls Jazz Club since September of 2007 and incorporates more than 9000 recordings and over 1000 musicians. 20 shows per week are added to the Archive on an average.
Subscriptions collected from members of SmallsLIVE are pooled and distributed directly to the musicians based on usage. The more you listen to your favorite musicians or band, the larger the percentage of the revenue share they will receive.
You can follow your favorite musicians directly, receive updates as to when they will perform and share this with your friends. Certain shows will be available for download for your personal usage.
By becoming a subscribing member of SmallsLIVE you will have a direct impact on the club and the artists that create the music nightly.
