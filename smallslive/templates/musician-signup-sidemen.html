You need (2) sidemen to clear your videos

On SmallsLIVE, in order to publish a video and start to monetize it, the bandleader must be the sole owner of the video. In order to make you the owner of the videos on which you were a bandleader, you need to ask each musician who played with you to "clear" the video. By clearing the video, they are transferring their copyright and ownership in the video (Master Recording) to you.
