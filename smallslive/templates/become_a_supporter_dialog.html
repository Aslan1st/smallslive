WHY BECOME A SUPPORTING MEMBER?

The mission of the SmallsLIVE Foundation for Jazz Arts and Education is to disseminate jazz music through recordings and live streams. Also to sponsor individual jazz projects, tours and educational classes and to subsidize operating expenses for Smalls and Mezzrow jazz clubs. By making a tax deductible donation to the SmallsLIVE Foundation you will get access to our extensive audio/video archive and directly support the musicians and the clubs
