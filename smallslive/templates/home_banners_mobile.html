Archive

Every show at Smalls + Mezzrow has been recorded and archived since 2007. Supporting members enjoy unlimited access to our archive. Our musicians are paid royalties through our unique revenue share system.

Livestream

Our live stream has expanded our 60 seat clubs into a worldwide community of jazz fans and supporters. Sign up for free to watch our live streams each night.

Mission

The Smallslive foundation is a not-for-profit organized for the purpose of the dissemination of jazz music through our archive, live streams, sponsorship of individual jazz projects & tours, education, and live performances at Smalls &Smezzrow. we are supported by our members through tax-deductible donations.

WHY BECOME A SUPPORTING MEMBER?

The mission of the SmallsLIVE foundation for jazz arts and education is to disseminate jazz music through recordings and live streams. We sponsor individual jazz projects, tours and educational classes, and subsidize operating expenses for Smalls + Mezzrow jazz clubs. By making a tax deductible donation to the SmallsLIVE Foundation you will get access to our extensive audio/ video archive and directly support the musicians and the clubs.
